WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

WE TEACH THEM THE RIGHT WAY

The Rising People Welfare Society aims to build a better living ecosystem to the underprivileged children in New Delhi by providing them with the right education and care at the right time in life.

TRPWS is a joint programme with the mission to empower and support underprivileged children by ensuring that every child has at least 1 adult assigned to them.

about-us



Our NGO is a club formed youngsters whose main motto is social service. In this organisation everyone lives for one another and everybody lives for a cause. Itâ€™s a non profit organisation founded in 2012 with an employee margin of 2 to 10 main people. We are specialised in NGO and teaching.

This Non Governmental Organisation (NGO) was formed by a group of young people who wanted to get a change in society. It was started by three people namely Avleen Kaur, Kuwarjeet Singh and Harmandeep Kaur.

It was started unstructured in a small room in Shyam Nagar Basti, Subhash Nagar, NewDelhi. It had only 3 peoples and 10 students to start with but the gradually it took pace and team of 40 peoples was formed. The NGO stated providing internships so as to give other people chance to serve the society for a good cause.

The NGO works for students who are within age limit of nursery to intermediate (12 th ).

In 2015 the NGO started trips to places where students can learn. They had three trips a year. Air force and Railway museum were the highlights of the year. A swimming pool was also provided for the children with all measures of hygiene taken care of.

In 2016 the trip was to science museum. By 2017 50-60 students were part of this NGO.

By 2020 before lockdown 90 students were part of the NGO.
